Welcome to NHTSA Crash Reporting



According to the Essurance Study, 77% of Drivers in the United States have been in at least one car accident in their lives and that number is rising. In 2022, road crashes resulted in 38,000 people losing their lives each year and is the leading cause of death in the country.


NHTSA created a Standing Order so that all crashes using Automated Driving Systems were reported. The first remote-piloted cars were debuted by General Motors at the 1939 Worlds Fair. This eventually led to the first autonomous (self-driving) car prototypes in the 1980s after 30 years of testing.
